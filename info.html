asked questions

1. “How to subscribe?”

Visit our packages section on the homepage, select your package, nutritionist & duration then click add to cart. You will then need to fill in a questionnaire on your current lifestyle and your goals followed by your payment information

2. “What happens after I have completed the registration process and payment?”

You will be contacted within 5 to 7 days

3. “When can I contact my nutritionist?”

Each package has a different communication frequency: Diamond: 5 times per week (via WhatsApp & call) Gold: 3 times per week (via WhatsApp & call) Silver: 1 time per week (via WhatsApp only) The specific days & timing when you can reach out to your nutritionist will be determined on a case by case basis and will be shared with you on first contact
